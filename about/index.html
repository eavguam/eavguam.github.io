About Us

EAV is a student run club that strives to highlight the athletes of Harvest Christian Academy.

To accomplish this goal, they apply the use of social media across platforms such as YouTube, Instagram, and the EAV website. EAV was established on August 18th, 2022 and is based in Harvest Christian Academy, Barrigada GU. They are supervised by Joe Sarnie, Harvestâ€™s lead videographer.

EAV covers a wide variety of sports such as soccer, volleyball, and basketball. Aspects of EAV as a club include creating storyboards, selecting film locations, filming various sporting events, creating special effects, editing footage, syncing audio, and selecting/editing music to form high-quality videos. The club officers lead EAV video projects through pre-production, production, and post-production. The Film Crew is over production while editors take care of post production.
